Controls

  • Press c for a Cloud
  • Press d for Swap Leaves
  • Press f for Grass
  • Press g for Glow Stone
  • Press l for Lime Wool
  • Press r for Nether Rack
  • Press t for Oak Wood
  • Press w for Bricks
  • Press y for Yellow Bricks
  • Press up arrow key to move up
  • Press left arrow key to move left
  • Press down arrow key to move down
  • Press right arrow key to move right
  • Press Shift & p to increase the block size
  • Press Shift & m to decrease the block size
